About

Poocholamadu is a village in Kannamangalam in the Malappuram district, state of Kerala, India. It is part of the Gram Panchayat government. It is also included in Vengara Assembly constituency and Malappuram Lokhasabha constituency.

Historical records show that the village had the most fighters against british in the present Vengara region.

Geography

Poocholamad is 2.065 km away from Vengara and 1.36816 km from Achanambalam town. The village shares borders with Cheroor on the east side, Vettuthod, Gandhikunnu on the south side, Muttumpuram on the west side, and Achanambalam on the northside.

The village is mainly a slope from west to east

Transportation

Options for getting to and around Poocholamadu.

Getting Here:

By Air

The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 15 km away.

By Train

There is a railway station in Parappanangadi, 20 km away from the village.

By Bus

There is bus service from Vengara and Kunnumpuram.

By Car

Poocholamad is 7 km away from NH66. And 2 km from SH72 (Vengara).The capital
city Malappuram, is located 17 km away from the village.

Nearby University
