Transportation

Options for getting to and around Poocholamadu.

Getting Here:

By Air

The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 15 km away.

By Train

Parappanangadi station is 20 km away from the village.

By Bus

There is bus service from Vengara and Kunnumpuram.

By Car

Poocholamadu is 7 km from NH66 and 2 km from SH72 (Vengara).
Malappuram, the district capital, is 17 km away.

Nearby University

The nearest university is Calicut University, 16 km away.

Getting Around:

From Poocholamadu
